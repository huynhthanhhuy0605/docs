## Welcome to Image to Frames

Image to Frames is a powerful Canva app that instantly converts any image into a custom frame. With one click, transform photos, illustrations, or SVG files into frames you can use directly in your Canva designs. No design skills needed - it's that simple.

## Quick start guide

<Steps>
<Step title="Install in Canva">
Add the Image to Frames app to your Canva workspace from the Apps panel.
</Step>

<Step title="Choose your method">
Use an image from your design, upload a file, or import from our Frame Library.
</Step>

<Step title="Convert">
Click convert - your image is instantly transformed into a frame. Fully automatic!
</Step>

<Step title="Use in your design">
Drag images into your frame to crop them perfectly. Resize and style as needed.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Important: Import frames correctly

<Warning>
When you download frames from our library, you must import them into the Image to Frames app in Canva. Simply downloading the SVG file won't work - the frame needs to be imported into the app to display correctly.
</Warning>
