Image to Frames is a powerful Canva app that instantly converts any image into a custom frame. With one click, transform photos, illustrations, or SVG files into frames you can use directly in your Canva designs. No design skills needed - it's that simple.

## How it works

Image to Frames offers three simple ways to create frames:

<Steps>
<Step title="Method 1: Use image from Canva design (Recommended)">
Tap on any image in your Canva design. This is the fastest way - no upload needed!

<Tip>
This method is recommended because it's the quickest and most convenient.
</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Method 2: Upload image or SVG file">
Upload a photo, illustration, or SVG file from your device. Perfect for importing SVG files and converting them to frames.
</Step>

<Step title="Method 3: Import from Frame Store">
Download frames from our library of 8,168+ premium frames, then import them into the Image to Frames app in Canva.
</Step>

<Step title="One-click convert">
Click convert and your image is instantly transformed into a frame. Fully automatic - no tracing or manual editing needed.
</Step>

<Step title="Use in your design">
Your frame is ready to use in Canva. Simply drag images into the frame to crop them perfectly.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Important: Import frames correctly

<Warning>
When you download frames from our library, you must import them into the Image to Frames app in Canva. Simply downloading the SVG file won't work - the frame needs to be imported into the app to display correctly.
</Warning>
