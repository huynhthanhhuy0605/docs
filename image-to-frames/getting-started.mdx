Get started with Image to Frames in just a few minutes. This guide will walk you through installing the app in Canva and creating your first frame.

## Prerequisites

- A Canva account
- Image to Frames app installed in Canva
- Image to Frames Pro subscription (required - free users cannot use the app)

<Note>
Image to Frames requires a Pro subscription. Free users cannot access any features. Students and teachers can get a free 1-year Pro subscription.
</Note>

## Install Image to Frames in Canva

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Canva">
Log in to your Canva account and open any design or create a new one.
</Step>

<Step title="Open Apps panel">
Click on "Apps" in the left sidebar of Canva.
</Step>

<Step title="Search for Image to Frames">
Type "Image to Frames" in the search bar and select the app from the results.
</Step>

<Step title="Add the app">
Click "Add" or "Use" to install Image to Frames in your Canva workspace.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Three ways to create frames

Image to Frames offers three methods to create frames. Choose the one that works best for your workflow:

### Method 1: Use image from Canva design (Recommended)

<Steps>
<Step title="Select an image">
Click on any image in your Canva design that you want to convert into a frame.
</Step>

<Step title="Open Image to Frames">
Open the Image to Frames app from your Apps panel.
</Step>

<Step title="Convert">
Click the convert button. Your image is instantly transformed into a frame!

<Tip>
This is the fastest method because you don't need to upload anything.
</Tip>
</Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: Upload image or SVG file

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Image to Frames">
Open the Image to Frames app from your Apps panel in Canva.
</Step>

<Step title="Upload file">
Click "Upload" and select an image (PNG, JPG) or SVG file from your device.
</Step>

<Step title="Convert">
Click convert. Your uploaded file is instantly transformed into a frame.

<Note>
This method is perfect for importing SVG files and converting them to frames.
</Note>
</Step>
</Steps>

### Method 3: Import from Frame Store

<Steps>
<Step title="Browse Frame Store">
Visit the [Frame Store](https://qrdynamic.xyz/imagetoframe-in-canva) and browse our collection of 8,168+ premium frames.
</Step>

<Step title="Search frames">
Use the search bar to find frames by tags like animal, map, heart, cute, or man.
</Step>

<Step title="Download frame">
Click download to save the SVG frame file to your device.
</Step>

<Step title="Import into Canva">
Open Image to Frames app in Canva and import the downloaded SVG file.

<Warning>
You must import the frame into the Image to Frames app in Canva. Simply downloading the SVG won't work - it needs to be imported into the app to display correctly.
</Warning>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Using your frames

Once you've created a frame:

<Steps>
<Step title="Frame is ready">
Your converted frame appears in your Canva design automatically.
</Step>

<Step title="Add images">
Drag any image into the frame to crop it to the frame's shape.
</Step>

<Step title="Adjust">
Resize, reposition, or style your frame and image as needed.
</Step>
</Steps>
