## How conversion works

Image to Frames uses automatic edge detection to convert images into frames. There's no manual tracing or editing needed - the process is completely automatic.

## Converting images to frames

### Method 1: Use image from Canva design (Recommended)

<Steps>
<Step title="Select image in Canva">
Click on any image in your Canva design that you want to convert into a frame.
</Step>

<Step title="Open Image to Frames">
Open the Image to Frames app from your Apps panel.
</Step>

<Step title="Convert">
Click the convert button. Your selected image is instantly transformed into a frame.

<Tip>
This is the fastest method - no upload needed!
</Tip>
</Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: Upload image file

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Image to Frames">
Open the Image to Frames app from your Apps panel in Canva.
</Step>

<Step title="Upload image">
Click "Upload" and select an image file (PNG, JPG, or SVG) from your device.
</Step>

<Step title="Convert">
Click convert. Your uploaded image is instantly transformed into a frame.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

### Image quality

✅ **Use images with:**
- Clear, defined shapes
- Good contrast between subject and background
- Sharp edges (not blurry)
- High resolution for best results

❌ **Avoid:**
- Very blurry or out-of-focus images
- Extremely low resolution images
- Images with too many overlapping elements

## Using your converted frame

Once your image is converted to a frame:

<Steps>
<Step title="Frame appears automatically">
Your converted frame appears in your Canva design immediately after conversion.
</Step>

<Step title="Add images to frame">
Drag any image into the frame to crop it to the frame's shape.
</Step>

<Step title="Customize">
Resize, reposition, or style your frame and images as needed in Canva.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Conversion didn't work">
Make sure you're using a Pro account. Free users cannot use the conversion feature. Also check that your image is in a supported format (PNG, JPG, or SVG).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Frame looks incorrect">
Try using an image with clearer edges and better contrast. The automatic conversion works best with well-defined shapes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can't upload file">
Check that your file is in a supported format (PNG, JPG, or SVG) and isn't too large. Try compressing the image if needed.
</Accordion>
