## Text to frames

Convert text images into individual character frames for creative typography designs.

### Video tutorial

Watch how to convert fonts and text images into frames:

<iframe
  className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl"
  src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/7nxLuAT5-sU"
  title="Convert Font to Font Frame Canva"
  frameBorder="0"
  allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture"
  allowFullScreen
></iframe>

### How it works

<Steps>
<Step title="Create text image">
Design or find an image containing text that you want to convert into frames.
</Step>

<Step title="Upload to Image to Frames">
Upload the text image to Image to Frames in Canva.
</Step>

<Step title="Convert to frames">
Click convert. Each character in the text is transformed into an individual frame.
</Step>

<Step title="Use in your design">
Use the character frames to create unique typography designs, word art, or creative text layouts.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Best for

- Creative typography designs
- Word art and text-based graphics
- Custom letter frames
- Text-based social media posts
