Yes, if you don't cancel during the free trial, your annual subscription will begin automatically after the trial period ends.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time during the free trial without being charged. You'll continue to have Pro access until the end of the trial period.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="I upgraded my subscription, but I still don't have access to premium features. What should I do?">
If you've upgraded but don't have access, try refreshing your Canva app or logging out and back in. If the issue persists, contact customer support for assistance.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I cancel Pro anytime?">
Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll continue to have Pro access until the end of your billing period.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to my frames if I cancel?">
All your frames remain accessible. You'll lose access to Pro features like unlimited conversions and the Frame Library, but existing frames in your Canva designs aren't affected.
</Accordion>
