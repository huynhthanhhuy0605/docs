## Downloading frames

<Steps>
<Step title="Select a frame">
Click on any frame you like from the library.
</Step>

<Step title="Download">
Click the download button to save the SVG frame file to your device.
</Step>

<Step title="Save file">
The SVG file will be saved to your downloads folder.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Importing frames into Canva

<Warning>
**Important**: You must import downloaded frames into the Image to Frames app in Canva. Simply downloading the SVG file won't work - the frame needs to be imported into the app to display correctly.
</Warning>

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Image to Frames in Canva">
Open the Image to Frames app from your Apps panel in Canva.
</Step>

<Step title="Import frame">
Click "Import" or "Upload" and select the downloaded SVG frame file.
</Step>

<Step title="Frame is ready">
Your imported frame is now available in your Canva design and ready to use.
</Step>
</Steps>
