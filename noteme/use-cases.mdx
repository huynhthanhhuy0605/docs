## Quick note-taking

Capture ideas and thoughts instantly, wherever you are.

### How it works

<Steps>
<Step title="Create note instantly">
Open NoteMe mobile app and tap "+" to create a quick note. Use AI assistance for faster creation.
</Step>

<Step title="Add content">
Type or speak your note. Add links, tags, or set a reminder if needed.
</Step>

<Step title="Save and sync">
Your note is saved and synced across all platforms. Access it from mobile, web, or Canva.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Best for

- Capturing ideas on the go
- Quick thoughts and observations
- Brainstorming sessions
- Meeting notes
- Voice memos

## Appointment reminders

Never miss important dates or appointments.

### How it works

<Steps>
<Step title="Create reminder note">
Create a note about your appointment or task.
</Step>

<Step title="Set date and time">
Set a specific date and time for the reminder. NoteMe will notify you at the right moment.
</Step>

<Step title="Get notified">
Receive notifications on your mobile device when it's time to remember.
</Step>

<Step title="View in calendar">
See all your reminders in Calendar view to plan ahead.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Best for

- Doctor appointments
- Meeting reminders
- Deadline tracking
- Birthday reminders
- Task deadlines
