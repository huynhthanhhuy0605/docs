## Overview

The NoteMe Canva App lets you create notes without leaving your design workspace. Perfect for saving project ideas, maintaining checklists, and storing reference links for each design project.

<Warning>
The NoteMe Canva App requires a Pro subscription. Free users can use the mobile app and web version at [noteme.qrdyai.com](https://noteme.qrdyai.com).
</Warning>

## Installing NoteMe in Canva

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Canva">
Log in to your Canva account and open any design or create a new one.
</Step>

<Step title="Open Apps panel">
Click on "Apps" in the left sidebar of Canva.
</Step>

<Step title="Search for NoteMe">
Type "NoteMe" in the search bar and select the app from the results.
</Step>

<Step title="Add the app">
Click "Add" or "Use" to install NoteMe in your Canva workspace.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Creating notes in Canva

<Steps>
<Step title="Open NoteMe">
Click on NoteMe in your Apps panel to open the note-taking interface.
</Step>

<Step title="Create a note">
Click "Create Note" or the "+" button to start a new note.
</Step>

<Step title="Add content">
Type your note content. You can add:
- Project ideas and inspiration
- Checklists for design tasks
- Reference links and resources
- Reminders for project deadlines
</Step>

<Step title="Save">
Click save and your note is stored. It automatically syncs to your mobile app.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Syncing with mobile app

<Info>
All notes created in the Canva App automatically sync to your NoteMe mobile app. You can access them from anywhere, even when you're not in Canva.
</Info>

**How syncing works:**
- Notes created in Canva appear in your mobile app immediately
- Edit notes in either location and changes sync automatically
- Reminders set in Canva will notify you on your mobile device
- Tags and organization sync across all platforms

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="NoteMe app doesn't appear in Canva">
Make sure you have a Pro subscription. The Canva App is only available for Pro users. Free users can use the mobile app and web version.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Notes not syncing to mobile app">
Check your internet connection and make sure you're logged into the same NoteMe account on both platforms. Try refreshing the app or logging out and back in.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can't create notes">
Ensure you have a Pro subscription active. If you're on a free plan, upgrade to Pro to access the Canva App.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
